There are many diffrent cards, and many more updates coming soon that will be adding in more cards. With that in mind, there are decks that have great damage and winning potential. There are offense decks and deffense decks and decks used for both.

We can't just worry about the type of card you want to use, we also have to keep in mind the elixir cost of the card. When a deck consists of too much elixir cost, you are vulnerable to counters. Though decks with minimum cost will usually consist of weak troops that can only withstand one hit. So a deck must have high health and damage cards with low health but multiple units cards.

Cards like musketeer, prince, knight have single target attack so the can be easily swarmed. Goblins, skeleton army, and minion horde are swarm units that easily take out tank units and single target units but have low health and can be killed with arrows or a fireball. Tank units like the giant, skeleton giant, and golem are slow and only target buildings but have high hitpoints. Then there are splash damage units like baby dragon, wizard, and bomber who can take down swarms and grouped pushes. Now there are also building cards. They spawn defencive buildings to counter enemy troops. Like the inferno tower which can take down tanks or tesla tower which can take down swarms. Some can be put on offence like the x-bow or the mortar. They can target building from your side of the arena.

Sparky

Slow moving unit that does high single target damage.

Three Musketeers

Shoots devstating singular damage. Great agianst single air units.

Wizard

Shoots fireballs with amazing speed for splash damage.

Fire Spirits

Kamikazes into towers blowing uo and cause high damage. Works great with swarm units.

Knight

A low elixir tank unit that can deal out moderate damge but can provide protection agianst towers and unit.

Witch

A fast attacking unit that spawns skeletons for swarm affect.

Ice Spirit

Single kamikaze unit that freezes units for a time.

Giant

A large tank unit with alot of health. Great for protecting pushes.

Musketeers

A single musketeer unit with high damage and long range capabilities.

Minions

Spawns aerial minions that attacks air and ground units. Classified as swarm units.

Archers

Average damage troops that attacks air and ground units.

Skeletons

Spawns a unit of low health troops with fast hit damage. Useless by itself but overpowered with many others.

Balloon

Aerial unit that only attacks buildings for high damage. Drops bomb when destroyed.

Valkyrie

Great troop with good area damage and good health. She can take down swarms and pushes easily.

Goblins

Low health but high damage troop with fast melee and rapid deployment. Great for tank units.

Bomber

Has high splash damage ability to stop swarms and pushes with support.

Spear Goblins

Just like goblins but are ranged troops with aerial attacking ability.

Inferno Dragon

A baby dragon with inferno tower abilities. Damage progresses as the attack gets longer.

P.E.K.K.A

Slow tank unit with high health and massive damage. Expensive to deploy but when supported it can be unstoppable.

Lava Hound

Aerial tank unit that attacks building and splits into small lava pups that attacks anything.

Dark Prince

Like the prince but instead does splash damage and has a shield.

miner

Ground unit that burrows underground and appears right by the enemy avoiding usual damage caused by crossing the bridge.

Princess

She is a splash damage unit decent health and great damage.

Mini P.E.K.K.A

A smaller and faster version of the P.E.K.K.A dealing massive damge with less health than the full counterpart.

Royal Giant

A basic giant that does push but fires a cannon from a distance.

Mega Minion

Single armored minion that does decent damage.

Bowler

Splash damage unit that does lane damage.

Prince

Massive damage unit that charges on a horse which causes more damage than a basic attack.

Skeleton Army

Spawns 23 units of skeletons that do swarm damage.

Baby Dragon

Aerial unit that attacks splash damage which can easily stop pushes and swarms.

Guards

Three fast attacking skeletons that have shields which act as an extra life.

Hog Rider

Fast heavy damge troop thats fast and can jump over the river. Only targets defencive buildings and towers.

LumberJack

Fast axe weilding troop that drops rage when defeated. Level of the rage depends on the spell you own in your deck.
